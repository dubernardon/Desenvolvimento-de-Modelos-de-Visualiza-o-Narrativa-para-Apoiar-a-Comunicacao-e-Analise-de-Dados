Annotated Graph: South America Population

The main objective of this visualization is to point out the best evidence on the population issue in South America through the annotation tool and the annotated graph, which has aspects of literacy, growth, migration, mortality and birth rates, population and population density of each country to study.

Annotated chart used to study narrative visualizations at the PUCRS Davint laboratory in the project "Development of Narrative Visualization Models to Support the Communication and Analysis of Open Government Data" .

Claiton Marques Correa doctoral project.
Data: https://www.indexmundi.com/map/
